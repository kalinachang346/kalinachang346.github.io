Women In Chess


Female chess players are a minority in the game. There are only 42 women grandmasters as of 2024, out of 2000 grandmasters total. A significant gender gap can be seen between women and men in Chess. There are stark differences between women and men in the sport. For example, there are a set of FIDE titles exlusive to women as well as chess tournaments.



Titles


Women Grandmaster (WGM)
Women Internation Master (WIM)
Women Fide Master (WFM)
Women Candidate Master (WCM)



*These titles are execulsive to women, however women can earn the general titles too.


Women chess players are held to lower standards. The minimum rating requirements are 200 points lower for women.
